## Key Discussions & Decisions

### Domain Scope
- Discussed whether to focus on modern music only or cover the full timeline including classical/baroque periods.
- **Decision:** Cover the full music history timeline. Classical/older periods are well-documented and manageable. Historical periods (Baroque, Classical, Romantic) will be treated as genres/sub-genres rather than a separate Era entity.

### Entity Model Discussion
The team discussed and defined the key entity groups and their subgroups:

**MusicalWork** (broad concept encompassing all types of musical creation):
- Recordings, Albums, Tracks, Singles, Double albums
- Classical compositions (works that predate any recording)
- This addresses the classical music problem: a composition like Beethoven's Symphony No. 5 exists as a MusicalWork with a compositionDate, separate from any later Album/Recording.

**Artist** (broad concept encompassing all music creators/performers):
- Composers, Producers
- Musicians (Singers, Instrumentalists)
- Bands/Groups

### Initial 5 Focus Areas for Ontology
1. **Genre** — what genre/style a work or artist belongs to
2. **Album** — collections of tracks, release information
3. **Title/Track** — individual musical works
4. **Date** — when works were composed, released, recorded
5. **Artist** — creators, performers, producers

### 10 Manual Competency Questions (Draft)
Sofia led the discussion. The team brainstormed 10 CQs based on the focus areas:

1. What date was an album released?
2. What genre(s) did a given composer/artist write in?
3. What album was a given track released in?
4. Who produced a given album?
5. Which performers played in a given track?
6. What producer worked on albums of a given genre?
7. Who plays what instrument in a given band?
8. How many awards has a certain artist won?
9. What subgenres emerged from a given genre?
10. Which musical works were composed in a given era, and how much time later were they first recorded?

These are initial drafts — Sofia and Davyd will refine them into specific, locked-in competency questions.
