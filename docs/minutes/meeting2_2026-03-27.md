## Key Discussions & Decisions

- **Domain scope:** Cover the full music history timeline (medieval to modern), not just modern music. Historical periods treated as genres rather than a separate Era entity.
- **Entity model:** Defined MusicalWork as a broad concept encompassing recordings, albums, and classical compositions. Artist encompasses composers, producers, musicians, and bands. This addresses the classical music problem where compositions predate recordings.
- **10 manual CQs drafted:** Album release dates, artist genres, track-to-album lookup, album producers, band members, producers by genre, instruments in bands, award counts, subgenre hierarchy, composition-to-recording time gap.
- **Data sources confirmed:** MusicBrainz + Discogs (structured), Wikipedia (textual). Cross-referencing pipeline verified across 5 test artists.
